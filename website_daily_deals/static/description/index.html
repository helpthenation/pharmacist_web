Website Deals And Flash Sales

Website Deals and Flash Sales module allows you to create the Deals and Flash sales for your customers.The discount on the deals depends on the price lists. If you have pricelist for product ,then you need to add that product in the deal for showing in the website. You can customize the view of the deals in website by your own.By using this module you can easily promote and enhance your business.
Salient Features
  • Deal can be created in very simple steps.
  • Discount on the deals is applied through price lists.
  • Discount on the product will be displayed in the website automatically after adding the product in a particular deal.
  • Apart from the discount other offers can be applied on the products also.
  • Admin have full control on customizing the view from back end.
  • Admin can customize the view from front also.
  • Deals can be shown both in Grid view or in the Slider view.
  • All deals will be displayed in a separate page(Deals and Offers).
  • A single deal is also displayed separately that will show products in that deal only.
  • Customize the messages you want to show in website by your own.
  • Set the default Configuration for all deals or for a single Deal.
  • It has many more amazing features described below.

Deals/Offers in website

Banner And Header

  • Editable header and banner of the deal.You can edit the text of the header from front and back end and also you change the banner from front and back end.
Product Prices And Discounts

  • The Product discount will be calculated automatically.Actual price , Discounted price and the amount discount will be shown in different colors.
Grid View of the products in daily deals page

  • The Deals Can be displayed in the grid view.Each deal product has a link its actual product.On clicking the name or image it will redirect to the actual product.
Slider View of the products daily deals page.

  • The Products in the deal can be also displayed in the Slider view.You can move back and forth by using the button in the slider.On clicking the Image or product name it will be redirected to the actual product.
Show Deal Titles

  • You can separate the deals by using the deal titles.Titles will be displayed when only products in the deal are displayed.The Message (before expiration) will be displayed before the amount of time you have selected.
Blurred Deals after expiry

  • When a deal is expired it will be displayed blurred or it can be deleted from the website.Also the message after expiration will be displayed after expiration.
Seperate page for each Deal

  • There is a separate page for each deal.This page will be displayed by clicking on the banner of the deal.It contains the banner and the products of the deal. Edit the view according to you in this page also.
Edit the view From front end

  • You can edit the View of the Deals from front end also according to you. Customize the grid view according to you change the size or move the deals according to you.
Show/Hide the Deals page in website

  • You can hide or show the deals page from the website by simply checking or unchecking this option.
