Odoo Marketplace Seller Wise Checkout

Odoo Marketplace Seller Wise Checkout is a module by which orders can split seller wise. This module creates different orders in cart for different seller products and manages them separately. Also, the customer can checkout and pay for each order separately.

Highlighted Features

  • Admin can provide seller wise checkout to customers
  • Admin can manage all the orders of a particular customer separately at backend.
  • Customers can buy different seller products separately.
  • Customers will not loose other selected products in cart even after completing payment of one order.
  • Customers can see all the orders in cart page at ones.
  • Customers can see all the orders in cart popover and can visit directly to that order from view cart button in popover.
  • Customers can view the seller profile page directly from the seller cart.
  • If customers click on the proceed checkout button from the cart page, then they will be redirected to the checkout page of the seller of the selected product or that order.

Odoo Marketplace Seller Wise Checkout

Sometimes customer add many products from different sellers in single cart but at that time want to purchase the products from single seller only. At this moment customer also want to allow other products from other sellers to stay there in cart, for later purchase rather than deleting them from the cart or adding them to wishlist.

With our marketplace seller wise checkout module admin can provide seller wise checkout to customers to help them to purchase the products from single seller at that instant and save other seller products in the cart itself for later purchase.
