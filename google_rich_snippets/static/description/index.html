Google Rich Results (formerly rich snippets)

Wrap up your website's results in neat, informative and attractive rich results on Google pages in a few clicks.

Features
  • It help Google better understand its context for display in Search.
  • You achieve better distribution of your content to users from Search.
  • This module adds Organization Data (Corporate Contact, Company Logo & Social Profile) in Shop & product pages.
  • This module adds Website Data (Sitelinks Searchbox) in Shop & product pages.
  • This module adds Product Data (Breadcrumb, Product Rating & Availibility status) in Shop & product pages.

Search Features supported by the module.

Google Search works hard to understand the content of a page. This module can help by providing explicit clues about the meaning of a page to Google by including Structured Data on the page.

  • Breadcrumb

    Navigation that indicates the page's position in the site hierarchy.

  • Organization Data: Logo , Corporate Contact & Social Profile

    Your organization's logo, corporate contact & social profile information displayed in the Google Knowledge panel.

  • Rich results (previously known as rich cards): Carousels

    Display your rich results in a sequential list or gallery in search results.

  • Website Feature: Sitelinks Searchbox

    A search box that is scoped to your website when it appears as a search result.

  • Product : Price, availability & rating

    Information about a product, including price, availability, and review ratings.

Expected results after adding Structured data for rich results

Note: There is no guarantee that your page will appear in Search results with the specified feature. This is because search features depend on many factors, including the search device type, location, and whether Google thinks the feature would provide the best search experience for the user.
